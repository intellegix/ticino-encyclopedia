Manner Adverbs

Manner adverbs in Ticinese describe how an action is performed. They characteristically follow the verb and are not subject to gender/number agreement, making them structurally distinct from adjectives.

ben﻿
 (well) — IPA: [ben] — Derived from Latin bene, this is the positive manner adverb. Example: At parlà ben﻿
